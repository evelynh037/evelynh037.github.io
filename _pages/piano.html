Piano Clips Played by Me

I started playing the piano in kindergarten. Preparing for the grade examinations each year meant summers filled with endless hours of practice. Every day from 1 PM to 6 PM, while other kids laughed and played outside, I was stuck indoors with a clock beside me and a deck of poker cards by my bench, counting how many times I needed to play each piece.

I once told my mom I’d smash the piano the moment I earned my Grade 10 certificate—that’s how much I hated it. But looking back, I realize it wasn’t the piano I despised—it was the discipline and effort it demanded.

Over time, I grew to love the challenge, take pride in my progress, and feel the joy that only music can bring. And most importantly? The piano is still there.
